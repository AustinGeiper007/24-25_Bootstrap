Responsive Floats

Float an element to the left or right depending on screen width, with responsive float class .float-*-left|right (where * is sm, md, lg, xl or xxl)

You gotta resize the browser to see this work

Floats right on small screens or wider

Floats right on medium screens or wider

Floats right on large screens or wider

Floats right on X-Large screens or wider

Floats right on XX-Large screens or wider
